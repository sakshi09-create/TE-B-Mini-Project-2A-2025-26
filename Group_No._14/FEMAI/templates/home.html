About PCOD/PCOS

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) and Polycystic Ovary Disease (PCOD) are common hormonal disorders affecting millions of women worldwide. Early detection and proper management are crucial for long-term health.

Our platform uses cutting-edge artificial intelligence to help identify potential risk factors and symptoms, empowering women to take control of their reproductive health.
